THE TRAVEL

Richard Orlinski travels from one end of the planet to the other, and his works are exhibited all over the world. He is particularly inspired by countries such as Japan. He marvels at this land of contrasts, which combines audacity and reserve, calm and chaos, naturalness and ritual, avant-garde and tradition...

He delights in discovering this archipelago of a thousand nuances, a thousand colors, where the boundaries between art and play tend to blur.

UNIQUE COLOR

During his last visit, the sculptor decided to create a vinyl adaptation of Kong freed from his chains. A sculpture like a toy, whose articulated arms, hands, torso and legs create a new work of art with each variation in position. Its color? Transparent, so that every shade can be reflected, and each reflection in turn creates an original work of art.

Do you own a Kong Toy in Japan? Your Kong Toy will always be unique, depending on the positions you give it and the colors that surround you.
